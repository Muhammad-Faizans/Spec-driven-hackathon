## Why I Use MDX for Technical Writing

Traditional blogs are great for storytelling, but engineering often requires:
- Interactive examples
- Live demos
- Embedded components
- Visual feedback

MDX allows me to bring **frontend engineering practices directly into documentation**
while keeping everything version-controlled and developer-friendly.
